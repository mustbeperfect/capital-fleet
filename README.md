# About
This is a plugin for Endless Sky with the Mega Freight Plugin. It makes several heavily customized superheavy capital ships and new pocket heavy warships available to purchase at Luna, Sol. The superheavy warships include the Claymore and Lucerne Class and the superheavy freighters including the K-Class and the FG-10. Their stats have been slightly changed. New shield values were determined by taking the ratio of hull to shields for the Republic carrier and Cruiser (heavy warships from base game) and scaling them up to the hull of the superheavy's and averaging those values. Additionally, all fighter bays, pocket light warship bays, and pocket medium warship bays have been converted to heavy warship bays to reduce micromanagement. The conversation rate is as follows: 

The new pocket heavy warships are the Dulle Griete class and Grose Bosche Class for missiles and close up combat, respectively. 

All ships have been kitted out with top of the line outfits from different races. The superheavy warships are outfitted for 20% defense and 80% offense. Their gun loadout is mostly missiles to be able to reach far away targets. Energy weapons make little sense on these ships due to their sheer scale and slow speed. The freighters are outfitted mostly for defense.

The operating cost on these are insane. Expect hundreds of thousands daily in salaries for each superheavy. Additionally, total missile refills if you are using Dulle Griet Class for your pocket warship will be about 500 million credits per superheavy 😭💀. I highly reccomend missile loadouts though as it delivers massive offensive capabilities without your ships having to be in the midst of the battle. Anyways even being a billionare won't cut it for these ships, you'll need to be a trillionare.
